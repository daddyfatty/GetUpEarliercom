Hartford Marathon Training Log 2025

August 16, 2025 - 22 Mile Bethany Run

Training Metrics:
Distance: 22.0 miles
Pace: 7:44 min/mile
Time: 2:50:09
Elevation: 1,329 ft
Calories: 2,584
Shoes: ASICS Gel-Nimbus 26

This was an incredible 22-mile training run through Bethany, Connecticut. The route took me through some beautiful countryside with rolling hills that provided excellent elevation training for the Hartford Marathon.

The pace felt controlled throughout most of the run, maintaining around 7:44 per mile which is exactly where I want to be for marathon pace training. The elevation gain of 1,329 feet gave my legs a great workout and will help build the strength needed for race day.

Nutrition and hydration were on point today. I used my usual fueling strategy with Huma gels and maintained steady energy levels throughout the entire 22 miles. Recovery feels good and legs are ready for the next training session.
